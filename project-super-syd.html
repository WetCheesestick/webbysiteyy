Super Syd

A coming-of-age short centered on sisterhood, imagination, and the emotional shift that comes with growing up.

RoleCinematographer
FormatNarrative Short
StatusCrowdfunding / Pre-Production
FocusPlayful camera language and color

Visual Intent

The cinematography balances wonder and vulnerability through vibrant palettes, dynamic movement, and grounded intimate moments between the two sisters.

Execution Notes

Camera Style
The camera shifts from playful energy in Syd’s imagined world to calmer framing in emotionally honest scenes.
Color + Texture
Bright practical accents and soft contrast are used to carry the tone of childhood imagination without losing realism.
Production Goal
Bridge pre-production to principal photography with a visual plan aligned across previs, shotlists, and lighting diagrams.
