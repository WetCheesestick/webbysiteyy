Aastry

A narrative project rooted in emotional perspective and restrained visual tension, designed to keep the audience close to character psychology.

RoleCinematographer
FormatShort Narrative
StatusCase Study Buildout
FocusLens language and movement rhythm

Visual Intent

The visual strategy emphasizes controlled movement, selective close framing, and practical motivated sources to place the viewer in a shifting emotional state instead of pure spectacle.

Execution Notes

Camera Approach
Alternated between observational wides and compressed close shots to preserve scene geography while heightening pressure in dialogue moments.
Lighting Approach
Built a soft-key foundation with practical accents so the image could pivot between calm and unease without abrupt stylistic shifts.
Post Pipeline
Protected highlight detail on set and shaped contrast in grade to keep texture in skin and production design.
